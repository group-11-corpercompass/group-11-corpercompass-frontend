Ethnic Groups and Language

Oyo State is predominantly inhabited by the Yoruba people, one of the major ethnic groups in Nigeria. The primary language spoken is Yoruba language, although English is widely used for education, business, and official communication. The Yoruba culture strongly influences the traditions, social interactions, and cultural identity of the people in the state.
